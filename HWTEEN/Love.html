Love

family, friend, and guardian

Family

We always say that family is the one who loves us the most; therefore, they will do anything to protect us, even under the condition that may be harmful to them. Take those two novels we read as examples.

  • Bod and his birth mother (she asks the ghosts in the graveyard to protect and take care of Bod)
  • Meg and Charles (she brings Charles back with all her love)

Friend

Accompany each other to conquer the difficulties and influence each other to get better in lives. "At home one depends on his parents; abroad, he needs the help of his friends." Friends are indispensable in men's lives because they will help a lot in one's life. Just like the main characters in the stories, they encounter some problems but finally, their friends will help them to solve them.

  • Bod and Liza (he is kidnapped by the pawnshop owner, and she helps him escape)
  • Meg and Calvin (when she doubts herself, he helps her find her own value)
